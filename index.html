YOU GO GIRL!

We are helping Girls to live more confident lives.Period!

You go girl washable pads provide comfort to women and girls. As an NGO we distribute our pads to at risk women and girls who live in underdeserved communitiess. We LOVE our product and want everyone to use them. They are completely sustainable and eco friendly. If you love the planet then you too need to use our washable pads. We also provide menstrual health education to young women, girls, boys, educators, community leaders and parents. You go girl manufactures and distributes washable pads to young girls and women ensuring that entering womanhood is a time of joy and celebration rather than a time of shame and pain. Each pack contains 6 washable pads. One pad is equivalent to 144 disposable pads over the 48 month period they last.
